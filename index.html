Key DIY Maintenance Instructions

  • Drivetrain: Keep the chain clean and lubricated. In wet, salty, or dusty conditions, perform this more frequently.
  • Wheels & Tires: Tires must be inflated within the range posted on the tire sidewalls (20–40 PSI). Rims should run true without wobbles, dents, or kinks.
  • Brakes: Brake levers must be firm and not touch the grips. If the system feels "spongy," the hydraulic brakes must be bled to remove air.
  • Bearings: Periodically check headset, wheel, pedal, and bottom bracket bearings for free movement; they should operate without excess play, grinding, or rattling.
  • Long-Term Storage: If storing for >2 weeks, remove the battery, store it in a dry, climate-controlled location (32°F–104°F), and check/recharge the battery to 75% monthly.
